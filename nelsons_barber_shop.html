Our Story

A Westside
Institution

Tucked away on North 19th Street in the heart of Colorado Springs' Westside, Nelson's Barber Shop has been a neighborhood fixture for decades. We're not a chain, not a franchise — just an honest-to-goodness barbershop where real craft is practiced every single day.

When you sit in our chair, you're getting more than a haircut. You're getting the unhurried attention of a skilled barber who takes pride in his work, remembers how you like your fade, and sends you out the door looking exactly right.

Whether you're a longtime regular, a new neighbor, or a dad bringing his son in for his very first cut — you'll feel at home here. That's the Nelson's way.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Walk-ins are always welcome at Nelson's — that's the way a real barbershop operates. We work on a first-come, first-served basis. If there's a wait, you're welcome to hang out in the shop. We do have a small number of appointment slots available for regulars who need a specific time, so give us a call if you need to nail down a window.
We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, as well as mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Cash is always appreciated and tips mean the world to your barber — it goes directly to the person who cut your hair.
Most haircuts run between 20–35 minutes depending on the complexity of the style. A beard trim add-on takes another 10–15 minutes. The Haircut & Shave Combo typically runs about 45–55 minutes. We don't rush — good work takes the time it takes.
Nelson's is a traditional men's barbershop — we specialize in men's haircuts, fades, tapers, beard work, and straight razor shaves. We don't offer coloring, styling, or women's cuts. For those services, we'd recommend finding a dedicated salon. But for any man's cut? We're your place.
We're at 732 N 19th St, Colorado Springs, CO 80904 — right on the Westside. There's street parking available directly in front of the shop on 19th Street, and typically plenty of spots nearby. We're easy to find and easy to park at. Give us a call at (719) 442-1887 if you have any trouble locating us.
