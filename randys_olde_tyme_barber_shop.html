Our Story

A Landmark on
West Colorado Ave

✦

Nestled in the heart of historic Old Colorado City — one of Colorado Springs' most beloved and storied neighborhoods — Randy's Olde Tyme Barber Shop has been a trusted anchor of West Colorado Avenue for years. This stretch of road tells the tale of the West, and Randy's tells it in every razor-clean shave and scissor-sharp cut.

We believe in doing things the old way — the right way. No appointments rushed, no corners cut. Just genuine, unhurried craftsmanship delivered by hands that know their trade. From the straight razor on the strop to the warm lather in the mug, every visit feels like stepping back in time.

Whether you're a longtime local or just passing through the Pikes Peak region, pull up a chair. You'll leave looking sharp and feeling like part of something that matters.

Got Questions?

The Curious Cowpoke's Guide

✂

Everything you need to know before you mosey on in.

Walk-ins are always welcome at Randy's — that's the old-fashioned way and we wouldn't have it any other way. If you want to plan ahead or check wait times, give us a ring at (719) 328-1135. We'll let you know how busy it is so you can time your visit right.
Our straight razor shave is the full traditional treatment: hot lather applied generously, a warm towel wrap to open the pores, a careful straight razor shave against the grain for maximum smoothness, a cool towel finish, and a touch of aftershave. It's a genuine luxury experience for just $20 — a ritual that used to define every man's week.
We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays — the Lord's day and the barber's day of rest. We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before close to ensure we can take care of you properly.
Absolutely — we love having the next generation in the chair. Our kid's cut is priced at $14 and is designed for children ages 12 and under. We're patient and friendly, and we've had plenty of practice making the experience enjoyable for young ones who might be nervous about the barber shop.
We're at 3436 W Colorado Ave, Colorado Springs, CO 80904 — right in the heart of historic Old Colorado City. Street parking is generally available along West Colorado Avenue, and the neighborhood is very walkable. Look for us on the main drag — we're hard to miss. If you need directions, give us a call at (719) 328-1135.
