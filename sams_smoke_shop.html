Our Story

Fountain's Finest Smoke Shop & Hookah Lounge

We're not just a smoke shop — we're your neighborhood hookah lounge. Locally owned and operated in the heart of Fountain, Colorado, Sam's brings together a warm, welcoming atmosphere with an extensive selection of premium smoking products.

Whether you're a seasoned hookah enthusiast looking for the finest shisha blends, or you're exploring our curated collection of glass pipes, vaporizers, and accessories — our friendly, knowledgeable staff is here to guide you every step of the way.

  • Hookah Lounge On-Site
    Kick back and enjoy a premium hookah session in our comfortable, beautifully appointed lounge space.
  • Vast Product Selection
    From top-brand shisha and glass pipes to vaporizers, CBD products, and all your favorite accessories.
  • Community-Centered
    We built this place for the Fountain community — fair prices, real service, no pretentiousness.
  • Expert Staff
    Our team genuinely loves what we do. Ask us anything — we're here to help, not just sell.

Hookah Sessions
