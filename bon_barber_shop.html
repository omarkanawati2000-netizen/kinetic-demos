Our Story

A Neighborhood Institution

"A great haircut isn't just about looking good — it's about feeling like yourself again."

Nestled on North Wahsatch Avenue in the heart of Colorado Springs, Bon Barber Shop has been a trusted gathering place for men of all ages for decades. We believe in the art of the traditional barbershop — where skilled hands, good conversation, and honest prices come together.

Whether you're a longtime regular or stepping through our door for the first time, you'll receive the same unhurried, attentive service that has kept Colorado Springs coming back year after year.

Frequently Asked

No appointment needed! Bon Barber Shop operates on a walk-in basis. Simply come in during our business hours (Monday–Saturday, 9AM–6PM) and we'll take care of you in the order you arrive. For large groups or special occasions, feel free to call ahead.

We accept cash and all major credit/debit cards. While we appreciate tips — and our barbers certainly do too — they are always at your discretion.

A standard Men's Haircut takes about 20–30 minutes. The Works package (haircut + beard + shave) typically takes 45–60 minutes. We never rush — quality takes the time it takes.

Absolutely. Our Kids Cut ($15) is designed for boys 12 and under. Our barbers are patient, kind, and experienced with kids who might be nervous about getting their hair cut. First haircuts are always a special event here.
