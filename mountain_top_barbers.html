Our Story

Rooted in the Rockies

Mountain Top Barbers was built on one simple idea: every man deserves a great cut at a fair price. Nestled in Fountain, Colorado — with the Rockies as our backdrop — we've been keeping the men of the Front Range looking sharp since 2018.

We're proud to serve Fountain, Colorado Springs, and the surrounding mountain communities. Whether you're a soldier from Fort Carson, a local tradesman, or just passing through — you're welcome in our chairs.

No frills. No attitude. Just skilled hands, quality product, and the kind of honest, straightforward service that Colorado is known for.

Got Questions?

We've Got Answers

Nope! Walk-ins are always welcome at Mountain Top Barbers. We operate on a first-come, first-served basis, so you can simply show up during our business hours and we'll get you in the chair. If you want to minimize wait time, mornings on weekdays tend to be the least busy.
We're open Monday through Friday from 9AM to 7PM, and Saturdays from 9AM to 5PM. We are closed on Sundays. Holiday hours may vary — follow us on social media or give us a call to confirm.
We accept cash, all major credit cards, and contactless payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). We appreciate cash tips for our barbers, but card tips are also welcome!
