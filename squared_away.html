Our Story

Born from a
passion for the
Standard

Squared Away opened its doors in January 2026 right here in Fountain, Colorado — steps from the gate, built for the mission. We're part of the same military community we serve: Fort Carson soldiers, Peterson AFB Airmen, and the broader Colorado Springs military family.

We understand what it means to look sharp on PT day, pass a uniform inspection, or walk into a formal event with dress blues pressed to perfection. Every stitch we place is made with military standards in mind — because we know how much it matters.

Whether it's a quick name tape or a full dress uniform prep, we treat every job like orders: with precision, speed, and pride.

What We Do

Services

Most Popular
Uniform Alterations & Tailoring

Expert alterations for Army ACU/OCP, Air Force ABU/OCP, and all service uniforms. Precision fit that meets regs.

Rank & Patch Sewing

Proper placement and secure attachment of rank insignia, unit patches, and combat patches per military regulation.

Name Tape & Branch Tape Service

Professional sewing of name tapes and branch tapes. Straight, centered, regulation-compliant every time.

Hemming & Fitting

Trouser hemming, sleeve adjustments, and full-garment fitting so your uniform looks tailored and professional.

Unit Patches & Insignia

Sewing of divisional patches, Special Forces tabs, Ranger tabs, and all authorized unit insignia.

Dress Uniform Preparation

Full dress uniform prep for formal events, promotions, retirements, and ceremonies. You'll stand at attention and look the part.

Custom Sewing & Embroidery

Beyond uniforms — morale patches, custom embroidery, unit gifts, and specialty sewing projects.
