Our Story

CRAFTED WITH SKILL.
BUILT ON TRUST.

Nestled in the heart of north Colorado Springs, Academy Barber Shop has been the neighborhood's trusted grooming destination for over a decade. We believe every man deserves a sharp, confident look — and we're here to deliver it every single time.

From classic cuts to modern fades, our experienced barbers combine old-school craftsmanship with fresh technique. Whether you're prepping for a big interview or just maintaining your style, we've got you covered.

Precision Cuts Every Time
Each cut is tailored to your face shape, hair type, and personal style — no cookie-cutter results.
Clean, Sanitary Environment
We maintain the highest hygiene standards. Every tool is sterilized between clients, every time.
Friendly, Professional Staff
Our barbers are passionate, knowledgeable, and always ready to chat or give advice.
