Our Story

More Than Just a Nail Salon

At Just Nails, we believe every visit should leave you feeling refreshed, confident, and beautiful. Located in the heart of Fountain, CO, our salon is a sanctuary of style and self-care.

Hospital-Grade Sanitation

We sterilize every tool between clients and use disposable liners for pedicure bowls. Your health is our highest priority.

Certified, Skilled Technicians

Our licensed nail artists bring years of experience and a passion for detail — from classic polish to intricate nail art.

A Welcoming Atmosphere

Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and exceptional service every time.
