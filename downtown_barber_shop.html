Call Us

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

+
Walk-ins are absolutely welcome — no appointment needed. Just come in and we'll get you in the chair. If you prefer to plan ahead, give us a call at (720) 300-5313 and we'll sort out a time that works for you.

What are your hours and where exactly are you located?

+
We're at 30 E Willamette Ave, Colorado Springs, CO 80903 — right in downtown. Our hours are Monday–Friday 9AM to 6PM, Saturday 9AM to 4PM. We're closed Sundays.

What's included in the Hot Towel Shave?

+
The hot towel shave ($22) includes a warm pre-shave steam towel to open the pores, expert straight razor shaving, and a cool-down towel to close the skin afterward. It's the full old-school barbershop experience — relaxing, precise, and incredibly smooth.

What's the difference between the Cheap Haircut Special and the regular Men's Haircut?

+
The Cheap Haircut Special ($15) is a quick, clean, no-frills cut — great when you need a tidy-up fast or you're watching the budget. The regular Men's Haircut ($20) includes a bit more consultation, detailed styling, and extra finishing work. Both are quality cuts — the special is just streamlined.

Do you accept cards or is it cash only?

+
We accept both cash and cards — whichever works best for you. No need to hit an ATM before you come in. If you have any other questions about payment, feel free to call us at (720) 300-5313.
