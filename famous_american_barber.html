Our Story

Proudly Serving Colorado Springs

At Famous American Barber, we believe a great haircut is more than just a trim — it's a statement of pride, confidence, and identity. Located in the heart of Colorado Springs at Voyager Parkway, we've built our reputation one sharp fade at a time.

Nestled near the proud military community of Fort Carson, we understand the standard of precision, discipline, and excellence. Whether you're a service member, a local professional, or bringing in the kids, our barbers deliver results you can stand tall in.

Colorado Springs is our home, and every client who sits in our chair is part of our American family.

Do I need an appointment?
▼
No appointment needed! We proudly welcome walk-ins every day. Just come on in during business hours and we'll take care of you. If you have a larger group or special event, feel free to call ahead and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you offer military discounts?
▼
Absolutely. We're proud to be near Fort Carson and have deep respect for our military community. Please mention your active duty or veteran status when you come in — we honor your service and show it. Ask about current military pricing when you visit.
What's included in The Full Service?
▼
The Full Service ($48) is our signature combo package: a complete men's haircut, a hot towel shave, and a beard trim — all done with the precision and care you expect from Famous American Barber. It's the complete grooming experience in a single visit. Hands down our best value.
Do you cut kids' hair?
▼
Yes! Kids are always welcome at Famous American Barber. Our Kids Cut service is designed for children ages 12 and under ($18). Our barbers are patient, friendly, and experienced with young ones who may be nervous in the chair. We make it fun.
Where exactly are you located?
▼
We're located at 12245 Voyager Pkwy, Suite 136, Colorado Springs, CO 80921. We're in the Voyager Parkway area, easily accessible and with ample parking. If you need help finding us, just call us at (719) 358-9142 and we'll guide you right to us!
