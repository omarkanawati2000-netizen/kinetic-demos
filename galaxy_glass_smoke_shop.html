Our Story

Locally Rooted,
Cosmically Curated

Galaxy Glass is Colorado Springs' premier locally-owned smoke shop — a destination for enthusiasts who demand quality without compromise. We're not just a shop; we're a community, a knowledge hub, and your trusted guide through the universe of premium glass and smoke.

Locally Owned & Operated

Proudly rooted in Colorado Springs since day one. We know our community because we're part of it — real people serving real neighbors.

Knowledgeable, Friendly Staff

Our team lives and breathes this stuff. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned collector, we'll find exactly what you need.

Premium Selection, Every Time

We handpick every product on our shelves. Only the finest glass, most trusted brands, and highest quality items make the cut.

What We Carry

Explore Our Galaxy

From premium hand-blown glass to top-shelf accessories — we carry everything your universe needs.

Premium Glass Pipes

Hand-blown artisan pieces, one-hitters, spoons, and chillums from top glass artists. Every pipe is a work of art.

Water Pipes & Bongs

Scientific glass, beakers, percolators, recyclers, and bubblers. Every size, style, and diffusion type in stock.

Rolling Papers & Wraps

Raw, Zig-Zag, Juicy, Elements, Backwoods, King Palm — every paper, every size, every flavor you're looking for.

Vaporizers & E-Cigs

Portable vaporizers, desktop units, e-cigarettes, and pod systems. Top brands, full accessories, and expert guidance.

CBD Products

Tinctures, gummies, topicals, and flower from trusted hemp brands. Natural wellness, Colorado-approved.

Hookah & Shisha

Traditional and modern hookahs, premium shisha tobacco in hundreds of flavors, coals, and all the accessories to go with them.

Grinders & Accessories

Aerospace aluminum grinders, roach clips, storage jars, dab tools, torches, and every must-have accessory in the cosmos.

Incense & Candles

Nag champa, Satya, Hem, and premium candles to set the perfect atmosphere. Scents from around the universe.

You must be 21 years of age or older to enter and shop at Galaxy Glass. We strictly enforce this policy and require a valid, government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID) upon entry. No exceptions — this is Colorado law and we take it seriously.
