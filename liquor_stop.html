About Us

More Than Just a Liquor Store

At Liquor Stop, we're your neighbors first and retailers second. Nestled right here in Colorado Springs, we've built our store around one simple belief — everyone deserves access to a world-class selection without driving across town or paying inflated prices.

From rare single-malt Scotches and small-batch bourbons to natural wines and cold-craft beers, our shelves are curated with care. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, stocking up for the weekend, or searching for that perfect gift — we've got you covered.

Locally Owned & Operated

Colorado Springs is home — we're invested in this community and proud to serve our neighbors.

Curated Selection

Hand-picked spirits, wines, and craft beers from around the world. Quality over quantity — every bottle tells a story.

Knowledgeable & Friendly Staff

Our team genuinely loves what they do. Come in for a recommendation — leave with something you'll love.
