Our Story

Where Craft Meets Character

Luxe Salon was born from a singular belief: that visiting a salon should feel like stepping into a different world — one of quiet luxury, attentive expertise, and transformative artistry. Founded in 2014 by master colorist Elara Voss, we set out to create Boulder's most refined hair atelier.

Our philosophy is simple: every strand of hair tells a story. Our role is to help you tell yours beautifully. We blend time-honored European techniques with modern innovation, using only the finest sustainable, professional-grade products that honor both your hair and the planet.

We believe in slow beauty — taking the time to truly understand your lifestyle, your vision, and your hair's unique character. No rushed appointments. No cookie-cutter results. Only custom, considered transformations that leave you feeling extraordinary.

Questions

Frequently Asked

You can book in three ways: use the booking form below on this page, call us directly at (720) 555-4321 during business hours, or send us a message on Instagram @luxesalonboulder. We recommend booking at least 5–7 days in advance for color services, and 2–3 weeks for bridal appointments. Walk-ins are welcome for blowouts when availability permits.
We ask for at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made within 24 hours of your appointment will incur a 25% service fee. No-shows are charged 50% of the booked service. We understand life happens — please reach out as soon as possible and we'll always do our best to accommodate you.
We exclusively use professional-grade, sustainable products. Our color lines include Goldwell, Olaplex, and Schwarzkopf Professional. For treatments, we are certified Brazilian Blowout and Olaplex partners. Our retail shelf carries Oribe, Kerastase, and R+Co — all sulfate-free, cruelty-free, and thoughtfully formulated. We are proud to be committed to an eco-conscious beauty practice.
Service duration varies by the complexity of your request. A single-process all-over color takes approximately 1.5–2 hours. Highlights and partial balayage range from 2–3 hours, while full balayage and color corrections can require 3–5 hours. During your consultation, we'll give you an accurate time estimate so you can plan your day accordingly.
Absolutely. We offer complimentary 15-minute consultations for all new clients and for anyone considering a significant change such as a color correction, color transformation, or keratin treatment. Bridal clients receive a dedicated 45-minute consultation included in their package. Simply request a consultation when booking and we'll pair you with the perfect stylist for your goals.
