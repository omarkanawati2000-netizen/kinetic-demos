About Us

We Love Pets As Much As You Do

Annie's Pet Salon was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves to feel loved, comfortable, and beautiful. Founded by Annie — a lifelong animal lover and certified master groomer — our salon is a place where pets don't just get groomed, they get pampered.

Located in the heart of Colorado Springs at 4853 Barnes Rd, we serve dogs, cats, and other furry friends with the same care and tenderness we'd give our own beloved pets. Our team of certified, compassionate groomers takes the time to understand each pet's unique needs, temperament, and coat type.

Stress-Free Environment Calm, gentle handling techniques to keep your pet relaxed throughout their visit.
Certified & Experienced All our groomers are professionally certified with years of hands-on experience.
Safe, Natural Products We only use pet-safe, eco-friendly shampoos and conditioners — gentle on skin and coat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before bringing your furry friend to see us.

It depends on your dog's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Generally, most dogs benefit from grooming every 4–8 weeks. Short-haired breeds may go longer between visits, while long-haired or curly-coated breeds (like Poodles or Doodles) typically need grooming every 4–6 weeks to prevent matting and keep their coat healthy.
We recommend a short walk before the appointment to help your pet relax. Make sure your pet is up to date on vaccinations (we require proof for new clients). If your pet has any anxiety or skin sensitivities, please let us know when booking so we can prepare accordingly. No need to bathe them beforehand — that's our job!

Appointment duration depends on the service and your pet's size, coat type, and temperament. A basic bath & brush for a small dog may take 1–1.5 hours, while a full groom for a large dog with a complex coat might take 2–3 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you book and notify you when your pet is ready for pickup.
