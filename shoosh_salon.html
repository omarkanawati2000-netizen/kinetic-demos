Call Us
Do I need an appointment, or do you take walk-ins?
+

We recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred stylist and time slot. However, we do accommodate walk-ins based on availability. Call us at (719) 321-7337 to check same-day openings.

What is the DevaCurl method and do I need it?
+

DevaCurl is a curl-specific cutting technique performed on dry hair, allowing the stylist to see your natural curl pattern and cut each curl individually. It's ideal for wavy, curly, or coily hair (types 2A–4C). If you have curly hair, we highly recommend it for the best results.

How long does a color service typically take?
+

Color services vary depending on the service type and hair length. Single-process color typically takes 1.5–2 hours. Highlights and balayage can take 2.5–4 hours. Keratin treatments are usually 2–3 hours. We'll give you a time estimate during your consultation.

What should I do to prepare for my appointment?
+

For cuts, come with clean, product-free hair if possible. For color, avoid heavy conditioning treatments 48 hours before. For DevaCurl services, arrive with your natural curl pattern (air-dried if possible). Bring inspiration photos — we love to see your vision! Most importantly, come ready to relax and enjoy.

Do you offer consultations before booking a color service?
+

Absolutely! We offer complimentary consultations for new color clients and for any complex color transformations. This lets us assess your hair's health, discuss realistic expectations, create a color plan, and give you an accurate price quote — no surprises. Call us to schedule yours.
