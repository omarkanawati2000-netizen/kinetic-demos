Our Story

More Than a Market —
A Colorado Tradition

In 1987, the Mollica family opened their doors in Colorado Springs with a simple dream: bring the authentic flavors of the Italian countryside to their neighbors. Over 37 years later, that dream has grown into a beloved community institution.

We source directly from Italy — aged Parmigiano-Reggiano, handcrafted salumi, extra-virgin olive oils, and artisan pastas that you simply won't find in any grocery chain. Every item on our shelves and every dish from our deli counter carries the same love and attention our family has poured in since day one.

"We don't just sell Italian food — we share Italian life. Come hungry, leave like family." — The Mollica Family
