Our Story

Denver's Trusted Roofing Partner Since 2004

Apex Roofing was founded on a simple promise: deliver the quality craftsmanship that Denver homeowners deserve, at prices that are fair and transparent. What started as a two-man operation has grown into one of the Front Range's most trusted roofing companies.

We've weathered Denver's notorious hailstorms, served thousands of families across the metro area, and built a reputation that speaks for itself — not through flashy advertising, but through referrals from neighbors who've seen our work firsthand.

Every project, from a simple repair to a complete commercial replacement, gets the same level of care and attention to detail. That's the Apex standard. That's who we are.

Frequently Asked Questions

Generally, if your roof is under 15 years old and the damage is localized, a repair is often the most cost-effective solution. However, if the roof is showing widespread wear — curling or missing shingles, granule loss, multiple leak points, or is over 20 years old — a full replacement typically makes more financial sense long-term. Our free inspection will give you an honest assessment with no obligation to commit. We always show you both options so you can make an informed decision.
In most cases, yes — standard homeowner's insurance policies cover storm and hail damage as a covered "sudden peril." However, coverage depends on your specific policy, your deductible, and whether the damage meets your insurer's threshold. We work closely with all major insurance carriers and can help you through the claims process. We'll document all damage with photos, prepare a detailed estimate that meets insurance requirements, and communicate directly with your adjuster if needed.
Most residential roof replacements are completed in a single day — from tear-off to cleanup. Larger or more complex homes (steep pitches, multiple gable lines, skylights, etc.) may take two days. We handle all permitting ahead of time so there are no delays once the crew arrives. Weather permitting, we schedule your project start date at the time of contract signing, and we'll confirm the day before. You'll never be left guessing.
We're certified installers for all major roofing product lines. For residential projects we offer architectural (dimensional) asphalt shingles, impact-resistant shingles, metal roofing (standing seam and metal shingles), synthetic slate and wood-shake look-alikes, and traditional 3-tab. For commercial properties we install TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, built-up roofing (BUR), and metal panel systems. We'll help you choose the right product for your budget, climate exposure, and aesthetic goals.
Every Apex Roofing project includes two layers of protection. First, you receive the manufacturer's materials warranty — typically 25 to 50 years depending on the product line chosen. Second, we provide our own Apex Workmanship Warranty covering our installation labor for 10 years. This means if any defect related to our installation appears within that period, we return and correct it at no cost to you. We also offer an extended labor warranty upgrade available at the time of signing.
